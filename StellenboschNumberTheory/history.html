History of Stellenbosch Number Theory Conferences

The Number Theory conference series at Stellenbosch University began in 1997 as part of a Volkswagen Foundation German-South African Partnership Project on "Arithmetic Geometry and Data Security". The series has included workshops, student seminars, and international conferences held both in Stellenbosch and Essen (Germany).

The series began as part of a Volkswagen Foundation Partnership Project between the University of Essen and the University of Stellenbosch. Following the project's end in 2001, the Stellenbosch group continued to organize regular workshops as part of their research activities.
