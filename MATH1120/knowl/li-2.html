Any other behaviour is called divergence. For example
\begin{equation*} \sum_{k=1}^{\infty}k = 1+2+3+\cdots \end{equation*}
and
\begin{equation*} \sum_{k=1}^{\infty}(-1)^{k-1}=(-1)^0+(-1)^1+(-1)^2+\cdots = 1-1+1-1+1-\cdots \end{equation*}
(this last example is actually tricky to define properly).
