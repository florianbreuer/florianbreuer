Theorem 18.2.3.

  1. For the \(2\times 2\) matrix \begin{pmatrix} a \amp b \\ c \amp d \end{pmatrix}=ad-bc.

  2. For the \(n\times n\) matrix \(A=\begin{pmatrix} a_{ij} \end{pmatrix}\) we define the minor, \(M_{ij} \) as the determinant of the \((n-1)\times (n-1) \) matrix obtained by deleting the \(i\)th row and the \(j\)th column of \(A \text{.}\) Then

    \begin{equation*} \det(A)=\sum_{j=1}^{n}(-1)^{i+j}a_{ij}M_{ij}\qquad \text{for}\qquad i=1,2,3, \end{equation*}

    or

    \begin{equation*} \det(A)=\sum_{i=1}^{n}(-1)^{i+j}a_{ij}M_{ij}\qquad \text{for}\qquad i=1,2,3. \end{equation*}
