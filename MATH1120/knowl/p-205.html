Definition 3.3.4.

The equation of a surface of revolution obtained by rotating the curve \(z=f(x), x\geq 0\) in the \(xz\)-plane about the \(z\)-axis will be
\begin{equation*} z=f(\sqrt{x^2+y^2}) \end{equation*}
