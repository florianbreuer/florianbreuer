Research

I work in Number Theory. More specifically, my main interst lies in Arithmetic Geometry, where I work on Elliptic curves, Drinfeld modules, Drinfeld modular forms and related topics. Other interests include Ducci sequences, orders of elements in finte fields and most recently quadratic unmber fields and the Cohen-Lenstra-Martinet heuristics.
