Theorem 10.4.2.

Let \(C_0, C_1,...\) be a sequence of convergents for a continued fraction. Then

\begin{equation*} C_0 \lt C_2 \lt C_4 \lt \cdots, \end{equation*}

and

\begin{equation*} C_1 \gt C_3 \gt C_5 \gt \cdots. \end{equation*}

Moreover, \(C_{2k} \lt C_{2j+1}\) for all \(k, j \geq 0.\)

Proof.
