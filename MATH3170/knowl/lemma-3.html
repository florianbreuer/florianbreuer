Lemma 3.1.9.

Let \(a_n\in\mathbb{R}\) be positive for each \(n\) and suppose that \(a_n\rightarrow 0\) as \(n\rightarrow\infty.\) Then the product

\begin{equation*} \prod_{n=1}^{\infty}(1+a_n) \end{equation*}

converges if, and only if, the series

\begin{equation*} \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}a_n \end{equation*}

converges.

Proof.
