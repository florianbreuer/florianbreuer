Lemma 3.1.8.

Let \(a_1, a_2,..., a_n\) be positive integers. Then for any real \(x\) we have

\begin{equation} \sum_{a_1^{k_1}...a_n^{k_n}\leq x} 1 \leq \left(\sum_{a_n^{k_n}\leq x} 1\right)\left(\sum_{a_1^{k_1}...a_{n-1}^{k_{n-1}}\leq x} 1\right)\text{.}\tag{3.1.1} \end{equation}
